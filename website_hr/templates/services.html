Our Services

Recruitment & Selection

As a reliable placement agency, we have built a strong database of candidates with diverse academic backgrounds and work experience. We specialize in placing candidates who have completed their post-graduation, as well as those with diplomas. Our database includes both freshers and experienced professionals, providing a wide range of options for our clients.

Assessment & Training

In recent times, the world has become a global village. Markets all over the world have become dynamic and highly fluctuating. To cope with the challenges and competitiveness in the world every organization needs the services of the trained persons for performing the activities in a systematic way. Madrigal Corporate Solutions believes in training and coaching candidates to achieve world class. We believe that there is a right fit for the right person, and it is of absolute importance that one makes the right decisions in both- personal and corporate avenues.

Organisational Mapping & Structure

Organizational mapping and survey are crucial tools that help organizations gain a comprehensive understanding of wages and salaries across industries and regions. By conducting surveys and mapping out the compensation structures of other similar companies, organizations can get insights into how their salaries compare to their competitors and make data-driven decisions on budgeting salaries.

Salary Survey

Our salary survey service helps organizations to stay competitive in the job market by providing accurate and up-to-date information on compensation and benefits packages for various roles and industries. We conduct customized surveys based on our clients' needs and requirements, and collect data from various sources, including industry associations, government agencies, and our extensive network of contacts. We provide comprehensive reports that include data on average salaries, bonuses, and benefits packages, as well as insights and analysis on market trends and best practices. Our salary survey service helps organizations to make informed decisions when it comes to compensation and benefits, and to attract and retain top talent in their respective industries.

Career Counselling

Our experienced and qualified career counselors work with clients to identify their strengths, interests, and values, and help them to explore various career paths that align with their goals and aspirations. We provide guidance on job search strategies, resume and cover letter writing, interview skills, and networking. Our goal is to help our clients make informed decisions about their career paths, and provide them with the tools and resources needed to succeed in their chosen field. We believe that every individual has the potential to achieve their career aspirations, and we are committed to helping our clients reach their full potential.
